Pong!

The goal of the game is as follows.

Simply score as many points as possible and prove your worth against either a real life opponent or an automated opponent.

When playing pvp mode:

Player 1 will control his block using the "z" and "s" keys.
Player 2 will control his block using the "up" and "down" arrow keys.

When playing pve mode:

Simply use the "up" and "down" arrow keys to move your block accordingly.

Control can be switched to the mouse by moving it around. To switch back to the arrow keys: press "a"
